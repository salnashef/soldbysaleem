Other costs to check with the seller
  • Mortgage prepayment penalty — if the mortgage is being paid out before maturity, the lender may charge an interest rate differential (IRD) or 3 months' interest. This varies by lender and can be significant; get an exact discharge statement from the lender before listing.
  • Mortgage discharge / reconveyance admin fee — typically $75 to $300, separate from any penalty.
  • Property tax adjustment — prorated between seller and buyer as of the completion date.
  • Strata fees, move-out fee, or estoppel certificate fee, if applicable.
  • Home / tenant insurance cancellation and utility final readings.
